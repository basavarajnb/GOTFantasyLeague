Gameplay

  • User logs in / Signs up using Facebook.
  • User selects 6 players from the available list of players.
  • User has to select 1 player from Level 1 , Level 2 , Level 4 , Level 5 each.
  • User has to select 2 players from Level 3.
  • User saves the team for that week.
  • User earns points for each action of the selected players in the episode.
  • After the episode is telecasted and the points are calculated, Top 2 winners will be announced for the week.
  • At the end of the season , all the points will be added and the final winner will be crowned KING OR QUEEN OF SEASON 7.
